Unit 1｜TOEIC 800 必備學習策略與計畫

Objectives / 學習目標

  • Understand TOEIC structure and target scores. 理解考試結構與目標分數。
  • Create a personalized study plan and time-management. 制定個人化計畫與時間管理。
  • Practice efficient Listening & Reading techniques. 練習有效的聽讀策略。

Mini Practice / 小練習

Q: What is the best strategy to improve Listening quickly? / 提升聽力最快的方法？
A: Practice shadowing (跟讀) and focused dictation daily. / 建議每天做跟讀與聽寫訓練。

Tips / 解題技巧

  • Don't spend too long on one question. 遇到不會的題目不要卡太久，先跳過。
  • Use elimination to increase accuracy. 使用排除法提高答題正確率。
  • Simulate real test conditions weekly. 每週模考一次，檢視答題速度與錯題類型。

  • Master core grammar patterns. 精通核心文法句型。
  • Apply words & grammar to workplace contexts. 能應用於職場情境。

High-Frequency Words / 必背單字 (Sample)

Word中文Example Sentence

Grammar Focus / 文法重點

Participles & Comparison / 分詞與比較級

  • V-ing (主動/進行): The manager, reviewing the documents, asked for revisions.
  • V-ed (被動/完成): The documents submitted yesterday were approved.

Mini Practice / 小練習

Complete: The workers ______ overtime to meet the deadline. (work / worked / working / workes)
Ans: worked

Tips / 解題技巧

  • Pay attention to collocations (搭配詞) like submit a report, hold a meeting.
  • Use context clues to choose tenses and prepositions.

10 題範例練習（LR 混合） / 10 Sample Questions (LR mixed)

  1. 1. The new software will be available ______ next month.
    A. early  B. earlier  C. earliest  D. ear
    Answer: A — adverb modifies availability. (副詞修飾)
  2. 2. The report must be submitted _____ Friday.
    A. in  B. at  C. on  D. by
    Answer: D — by + deadline. (截至某個時間以前)
  3. 3. Employees are encouraged to ______ questions during the session.
    A. raise  B. rise  C. arise  D. arouse
    Answer: A — raise questions = 提問。
  4. 4. Listening — (Part 2 style) "When will the shipment arrive?" — "It should arrive by Tuesday." What does "by" mean here?
    A. before or on Tuesday  B. after Tuesday  C. exactly Tuesday  D. never
    Answer: A — before or on Tuesday. (在...之前或當天)
  5. 5. The manager asked the team to _____ the draft report for review.
    A. finalize  B. finalized  C. finalizing  D. final
    Answer: A — base verb after 'asked to'. (要求去做某事用原形)
  6. 6. Reading — Choose the best word: "The meeting was postponed _____ the CEO's absence."
    A. because  B. despite  C. during  D. although
    Answer: A — because = 因為。 (因為...的缺席而延後)
  7. 7. Customers must agree to the terms and conditions _____ purchasing online.
    A. before  B. after  C. while  D. during
    Answer: A — before purchasing. (在購買之前)
  8. 8. (Part 3 listening) "We need someone who can handle client inquiries and schedule appointments." What is the primary responsibility?
    A. Financial reporting  B. Customer support  C. Product design  D. Data entry
    Answer: B — customer support (處理客戶詢問與預約)。
  9. 9. The advertisement highlights the product's durability _____ its sleek design.
    A. and  B. but  C. or  D. so
    Answer: A — and = 並列兩者優點。
  10. 10. The receptionist will _____ you when the interviewer is ready.
    A. inform  B. informed  C. informing  D. information
    Answer: A — inform (動詞原形)。
